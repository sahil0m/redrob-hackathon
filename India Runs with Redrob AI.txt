Overview

Are you ready to redefine the future of talent acquisition?

Recruiters are tasked with finding the perfect fit from oceans of profiles, but traditional keyword filters are simply not cutting it.

They miss the hidden gems — candidates whose true potential, intent, and subtle behavioral signals are lost in the noise.

There has to be a smarter way.

This challenge asks you to build an AI brain for modern hiring.

We’re looking for intelligent systems that can move beyond surface-level keywords to:

* Deeply understand context
* Predict relevance
* Transform massive talent pools into precise, actionable shortlists

Join us to build the next generation of predictive ranking engines.

⸻

Your Mission: The Core Objective

Develop a robust, workable Proof of Concept that doesn’t just filter candidates — it intelligently ranks them.

Your system should act as the ultimate AI recruiter.

⸻

Core Requirements

1. Deep Job Understanding

Interpret complex and nuanced job descriptions.

Your system should understand:

* Required skills
* Preferred skills
* Experience requirements
* Industry context
* Seniority expectations
